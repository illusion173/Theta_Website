Flight Crews are responsible for the safe and reliable operation of our fleet of Boeing 747’s and 767’s. Theta Airlines is a leading global provider of outsourced aircraft and aviation operating services. Theta Airlines operates the world's largest fleet of 747 freighter aircraft and provides customers a broad array of Boeing 747 and 767 aircraft for domestic, regional and applications.

More Requirements

  • Hold a current unrestricted ATP certificate with an airplane category multi-engine class rating and English proficiency endorsement
  • Hold a current FAA First Class Medical Certificate
  • Do you have the ability to pass a 10 year security background check and a DOT pre-employment test.
  • Have a minimum of 1000 fixed wing or 500 hours with a 121 carrier
  • Have a minimum of 500 hours of turbine time
  • Have a minimum of 1500 hours of total time
  • Legally authorized to work in the United States
  • Hold an FCC Restricted Radiotelephone Operator Permit

Benefits

  • Your job takes you to other cities – can explore on overnight trips
  • Do not need a college degree
  • Many advancement opportunities
  • Opportunity to meet new people
  • 401(K) retirement plan
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • Paid vacation and holidays
  • Discounts on cruises, car rentals, hotels, and other hospitality services
To send an application, please send an email, including your name, contact information, and resume, to apply2jobs@thetaairlines.com

Does getting paid to travel sound like your idea of a dream job? That’s exactly what our flight attendants do every day! Theta Airlines is looking for friendly and outgoing individuals to join our team of flight attendants. No previous experience is required, but successful applicants will be highly professional, enthusiastic, and committed to providing our passengers with the highest standards of customer service. As our flight attendants have the most in-person interactions with our passengers, they have the biggest opportunity to impact the customer experience. That means that we work hard to find just the right candidates to fill our flight attendant positions. The ability to successfully work as part of a team with pilots and ground crews is a must.

More Requirements

  • Must be at least 19 years of age;
  • Must possess a valid driver's license or state ID;
  • Must be legally eligible to work in the U.S. (possess proper working documents).
  • Must possess excellent communication skills and have a professional and conservative appearance;
  • Must adhere to uniform standards and guidelines with no tattoos or body art visible at any time while in uniform. Examples of visible tattoo locations include, but are not limited to, hands, arms, neck, and face;
  • Read, write, and speak fluently in English. The ability to read and converse in another language is a plus;
  • Must have the ability to walk and fit comfortably down the aircraft aisle while facing forward, fit quickly through the over-wing exit, and sit in the Flight Attendant jumpseat without modification or a seatbelt extension;
  • DOT required pre-employment drug test;

Benefits

  • Your job takes you to other cities – can explore on overnight trips
  • Do not need a college degree
  • Many advancement opportunities
  • Opportunity to meet new people
  • 401(K) retirement plan
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • Paid vacation and holidays
  • Discounts on cruises, car rentals, hotels, and other hospitality services
