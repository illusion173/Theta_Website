Become A Theta Member

Membership Benefits
The Theta miles program is convenient for frequent flyers based in United States East Coast and for passengers who often travel to and from New York City. Theta Airlines operates more than 1,000 flights each day to 50-plus destinations across the East Coast United States. By becoming a Theta miles member, you'll earn miles based on the amount you pay for your flight with Theta Airlines, Theta Airlines Connection or Theta Airlines Shuttle; you can also earn miles for flying with any of Theta Airlines' multiple partner airlines, including KLM, Air France and Virgin Atlantic, among others. Additional miles can be redeemed for free air travel on any participating airline or cashed in for cabin upgrades, unique experiences (such as concert tickets). You can also earn miles on everyday purchases if you've signed up for a Theta Miles credit card by American Express.
Sign Up
To sign up for a card, email customerservice@thetaairlines.com with contact information to start the process of obtaining a Theta Miles credit card.
